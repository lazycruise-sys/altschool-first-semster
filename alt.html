Noodles Recipe

  1. Pick 3 Indomie (60g) and cut the nylon
  2. Pour 300mL of Water into your choice pot
  3. Place pot on Fire, and allow water to boil for 2 minutes
  4. Put noodles into pot and allow to steam for another 5 minutes
  5. Put seasoning and other things to your steamed noodles, and allow to boil for another 3 minutes
  6. Place your noodles into your choice plate
